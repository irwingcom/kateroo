User Insight


Our company is developing a full service catering application to provide accessibility to party planning services. We are better because customers are able to customize the service they seek all in one application. Such an application will be helpful for party and event host because it saves them time searching for the right services based of their specific needs and purpose.

Problem Statement


Our company, Kateroo, is developing a full service catering application to provide accessibility to party planning services. A problem we have discovered is that users have trouble finding a full service catering app because most applications will only give a user limited amount of options in one place. How might we help users plan an event in a timely manner and improve the quality and selection?
